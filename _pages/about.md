I am a Research Fellow/Adjunct Lecturer based in <a href="https://www.monash.edu/suzhou">Monash-Suzhou</a> Campus and adjunct to Monash University Australia. I obtained my PhD degree from Monash University in 2020. Before that, I earned my M.Sc. (Computer Science) degree from The University of Hong Kong and B.E. degree (Information Security) from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics.

My research mainly focuses on the design and application of encrypted search and computation schemes, and resolves the data security and privacy issues in cloud systems via these schemes. I am also interested in the design and implementation of efficient and secure cloud systems for multidisciplinary  applications (virtualised network function, machine learning, etc.). Currently, my research is supported by an Australian Research Council Discovery Project led by <a href="https://xyuancs.github.io">Dr Xingliang Yuan</a>.
